About Ekhaa

It is Non-profit sector charitable institution, established by
a decision of the Council of Ministers to care for orphans
with special circumstances (loss of family).
The meaning of Ekhaa is derived from cooperation and brotherhood,
and from Surah Al-Hujurat 10 إِنَّمَا ٱلْمُؤْمِنُونَ إِخْوَةٌ} ‏ : The believers are brethren}

The basic services that the Foundation provides to its orphans are: Providing housing,
Training and employment,Feeding and subsistence,Education, Preparing for marriage, and more

their values

  • Achieving social and life stability for orphans

  • Improving the psychological and social health of orphans

  • Social and financial independence for orphans

  • Developing Ekhaa’s capabilities for financial sustainability
